Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does PhotoSwipe delete my photos immediately?
    No. When you swipe left, photos are moved to the "Review Bin". You must manually confirm batch deletion, which will then trigger the official iOS delete prompt. Even after that, photos stay in your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album for 30 days.
  • Is my data safe? Do you upload my photos?
    Absolutely safe. PhotoSwipe works 100% offline. We do not have servers, and your photos never leave your device. All processing happens locally on your iPhone's neural engine.
  • How do I cancel my Premium subscription?
    You can manage or cancel your subscription directly through the Settings app on your iPhone: Open Settings > Tap your name > Tap Subscriptions > Select PhotoSwipe and cancel.
