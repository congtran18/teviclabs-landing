Frequently Asked Questions

Will PhotoSwipe delete my photos permanently?

No. PhotoSwipe staging deletes go to a Virtual Review Bin first. When you confirm deletion, iOS will request permission exactly once. Deleted items are sent to the system "Recently Deleted" album, where they can be recovered for up to 30 days.

Is my privacy secure?

100%. All duplicate calculations, image scanning, and OCR features run completely offline on your iPhone's Apple Neural Engine. Your photos never leave your device, and no analytics or personal data is collected on external servers.

How does the Smart Energy system work?

Free users get daily Smart Energy to use the automated Review Bin. If energy is depleted, you can still delete photos manually without limitations (though iOS will ask for confirmation on every single photo), or watch a quick ad to top up.
