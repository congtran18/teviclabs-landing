Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At TEVIC, we believe your financial data is exclusively yours. SubSlayer was architected from the ground up to protect your privacy and adhere strictly to Apple's sandbox model.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data, subscription details, financial information, or OCR scans. We literally do not own any servers to store your data on.

2. 100% Offline & Native

All core functionality of SubSlayer, including our "Scan-to-Slay" OCR engine, utilizes Apple's native on-device Vision Framework. No images or text are ever sent to the cloud for processing.

3. iCloud Syncing

If you enable iCloud sync, your subscription data is securely synchronized across your personal Apple devices using your private iCloud account. We (the developers) have absolutely zero access to this data.

4. No 3rd-Party Trackers

SubSlayer contains NO third-party SDKs, analytics trackers (like Firebase or Google Analytics), or advertising frameworks. What happens on your device, stays on your device.
