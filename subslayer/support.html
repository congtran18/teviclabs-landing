Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does SubSlayer cancel my subscriptions automatically?
    No. SubSlayer tracks and notifies you when renewals are coming up. To actually cancel a subscription to a service (like Netflix or Gym), you must log into that specific service or manage it via your Apple ID Settings. We provide a quick shortcut to Apple's Subscription Management page inside the app.
  • How does the "Split Cost" feature work?
    If you share a $20 streaming plan with 4 people, simply enter the price and set the split factor to 4. SubSlayer will normalize the cost and calculate your actual financial burden ($5).
  • Is there an Android version?
    No. SubSlayer is crafted specifically for iOS, utilizing native Apple technologies like SwiftData, Interactive Widgets, and the Neural Engine for the best possible experience.
